🏗️ Master Blueprint: Agency Insights &
Owner Dashboard
I. Global Logic & Database Architecture
  ●​ Data Refresh: Real-time sync with the Financial Engine of the Itinerary Builder.
  ●​ Role-Based Access (RBAC): Agents see their own stats; Managers see their team; the
     Owner sees the entire agency, including sensitive Net Costs and Agency Profit.
  ●​ The "TTV" Metric: The system tracks Total Transaction Value (TTV) (total client
     spend) vs. Gross Profit (actual agency earnings).




II. Module Structure: The "Command Center" View
  1.​ Top Row (The Pulse): High-level "Counter" widgets (Total Sales, Active Leads, Pending
      Commissions).
  2.​ Middle Row (The Performance): Comparison charts (Sales this month vs. last month)
      and Agent Leaderboards.
  3.​ Bottom Row (The AI Intelligence): Kimi-generated insights and "Risk Alerts."




III. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Data Fields

1. Financial Performance (The "Money" Pillar)
  ●​ Gross Profit Margin: (TTV - Net Cost) / TTV.
  ●​ Commission Tracking: * Expected Commission: Based on booked status.
         ○​ Received Commission: Based on supplier payout.
        ○​ Pending/Overdue: Flagged if a supplier hasn't paid within 30 days post-travel.
  ●​ Average Booking Value (ABV): Helps identify if agents are upselling to luxury.


2. Operational Efficiency (The "Speed" Pillar)
  ●​ Lead Conversion Rate: % of Leads that move to "Closed-Won."
  ●​ Average Response Time: Time from Lead intake to first message (WhatsApp/Email).
   ●​ Proposal-to-Booking Ratio: How many AI-generated proposals are actually getting
      signed.


3. Agent Productivity (The "Team" Pillar)
   ●​ Sales by Agent: Total revenue generated per team member.
   ●​ Client Retention Rate: % of "Repeat Clients" assigned to each agent.
   ●​ AI Usage Score: How often the agent is using Kimi to generate content (helps you see
      who is working efficiently).




IV. The AI "Executive Assistant" (Kimi k2.5 Integration)

1. The "Monday Morning" Briefing
Every Monday, Kimi generates a 4-paragraph report for the owner:

   ●​ "Overall sales are up 12% this week, largely driven by a surge in Oaxaca bookings."
   ●​ "Agent Sofia has a 90% conversion rate this month—consider having her mentor the
      new hires."
   ●​ "Warning: Three suppliers in your 'Europe' category have pending commissions older
      than 60 days."


2. The "Trend Spotter"
Kimi scans all Lead destinations and Trip_Intent fields to detect shifts:

   ●​ "I noticed an 18% increase in inquiries for 'Wellness Retreats' in Tulum. You may want to
      add more Wellness-focused suppliers to the database."




V. Functional Requirements for the Developer

1. The "Commission Reconciliation" Tool
   ●​ Requirement: A dedicated screen where the accountant can check off commissions as
      "Paid" by the supplier.
   ●​ AI Task: Kimi automatically matches incoming bank statement descriptions to specific
      Itinerary_IDs.
2. Custom Date Filtering
  ●​ Requirement: Allow the owner to filter all data by "Travel Date" (when the trip happens)
     vs. "Booking Date" (when the money was paid).


3. Export Engine
  ●​ Requirement: One-click export to Excel/PDF for accounting (SAT compliance in
     Mexico).




VI. Database Schema (For the Developer)

Table: agency_targets
  ●​ id, year, month, target_revenue, target_margin


Table: commissions_ledger
  ●​ id, itinerary_id (FK), supplier_id (FK)
  ●​ amount_due, currency, due_date, status [Pending, Paid, Disputed]
